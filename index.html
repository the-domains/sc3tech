A wide range of solutions is available for transport operators, with many very useful tools and capabilities: however many (possibly even most) focus on new systems and capabilities requiring new equipment. In many cases, however, newer tools and approaches can be used just as effectively - sometimes even more so! - with assets that are already in service. Whether the requirement is for efficiency gains, quality improvement processes, or lifetime extension, the use of analytic tools for existing transport operations is an area that is often overlooked.

SC3 Technologies can help, with a range of experience from embedded systems design (for onboard and wayside data collection if required) to “back end” database and cloud architectures: there is no ‘one size fits all’ product for transport operators (any more than there is for, say, manufacturing industry), and the key asset of our focussed approach is the ability to offer innovative solutions with short timescales to the proof-of-concept stage. While there is a focus on the rail sector, the principles and systems apply equally to other modes of transport, from ferries to fleets of road vehicles.
